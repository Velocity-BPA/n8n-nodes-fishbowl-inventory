# Automate Your Fishbowl Inventory with n8n: Introducing Our New Community Node

We're excited to announce the release of our latest n8n community node: n8n-nodes-fishbowl-inventory! At Velocity BPA, we're committed to bridging the gap between powerful business systems and workflow automation, and this node brings Fishbowl Inventory's robust warehouse and manufacturing capabilities directly into your n8n workflows.

## The Challenge: Disconnected Inventory Systems

Manufacturers and warehouses rely on Fishbowl Inventory to manage complex operations—from tracking raw materials to fulfilling orders. However, connecting Fishbowl to other business systems often requires expensive custom development or manual data entry. This leads to data silos, delayed responses, and missed opportunities for process optimization.

## The Solution: Seamless Fishbowl Automation

Our new Fishbowl Inventory node eliminates these barriers by enabling you to integrate Fishbowl directly into your n8n automation workflows. Whether you need to sync inventory levels with your e-commerce platform, trigger purchase orders based on stock thresholds, or generate custom reports combining Fishbowl data with other systems, this node makes it possible without writing complex code.

## Key Features

- **Comprehensive API Access**: Interact with products, sales orders, purchase orders, customers, and more
- **Real-time Inventory Monitoring**: Trigger workflows based on inventory changes
- **Flexible Operations**: Create, read, update, and delete records across Fishbowl entities
- **Seamless Integration**: Connect Fishbowl with CRMs, ERPs, e-commerce platforms, and hundreds of other n8n-supported services
- **Authentication Support**: Secure connection management with Fishbowl's API

## Getting Started

Installing the Fishbowl Inventory node is simple. In your n8n instance, navigate to Settings > Community Nodes and search for the package, or install it via npm:


npm install n8n-nodes-fishbowl-inventory


Once installed, you'll find the Fishbowl Inventory node in your n8n node palette, ready to drag and drop into your workflows.
